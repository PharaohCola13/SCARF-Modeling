    Was able to locate NASA research on the application of ITM models on the lunar surface.

    Lunar Approximations
    1. Ground Conductivity was approximated to be 0.0001 S/m
    2. The Dielectric constant was approximated to be 3.

    The comparison between the Longley-Rice, ITM, and ITWOM yielded results that favored the ITWOM model at distances <20 km. It was noted that the ITM model did not do so well in terms of line of sight measurements compared to the alternate models.
